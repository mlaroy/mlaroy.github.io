About Michael

I began my developer's journey BCIT's Technical Web Designer program, which is focused on training well-rounded developers with a focus on the front-end, complete with training in design and user experience. Ever since, I have been hooked on creating accessible, beautiful web experiences in a variety of platforms and frameworks.

I have completed projects in WordPress, Drupal, Shopify, Laravel, and other platforms. I also have experience using modern JavaScript tools and frameworks like Node, Webpack, Vue, and React. Lately, I've been looking at Ruby on Rails, React Native, as well as building skills for Alexa.

Currently, I am engaged full time with Engine Digital in Vancouver, British Columbia.

About this site

This site was designed in the browser to be a standalone single page site, optimized for speed and accessibility. It was developed with Webpack, Tailwind CSS, Vue JS, and plain 'ol vanilla JavaScript.
